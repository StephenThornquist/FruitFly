- Learning how to PCR with SXZ. Starting from before, where you have your 2x concentrated single primers (one L and one R):
    1) Make 100 uL each of 1:1 ratio of L and R primers for each reaction
    2) Make mastermix of Phusion enzyme, dNTPs, and water
    3) Mix primers with mastermix in a separate tube for each reaction
    4) Program PCR cycler according to annealing temp of your primers and with the duration of each step according to the length of the primer (and speed of the enzyme)
    5) Run it again with the attB primers to get the complete elemnt to insert
    6) Run gel to validate amplification of the correct fragment.
